Lord Krishna

About Lord Krishna

Lord Krishna is a major deity in Hinduism. He is worshipped as the eighth avatar of the god Vishnu and also as the supreme God in his own right. Krishna is known for his various roles during his life, including his childhood as a mischievous cowherd boy, his role as a friend and charioteer in the epic Mahabharata, and as the speaker of the Bhagavad Gita.
